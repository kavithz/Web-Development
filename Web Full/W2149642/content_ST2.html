Introduction

Quality education is one of the most powerful tools for achieving sustainable development and improving lives. It ensures that everyone has access to inclusive, equitable, and lifelong learning opportunities. Quality education empowers individuals with knowledge, skills, values, and attitudes to live with dignity, contribute to society, and promote sustainable development.

Causes

Families in poverty may not afford school fees, books, or uniforms.Poorly equipped schools, especially in rural areas, limit learning.In some regions, girls are denied education.

Effects

Lack of skills limits job opportunities.Education gaps widen between rich and poor.Poor education weakens the economy and innovation.

Solutions and Actions

Improve facilities, training, and resources.Support marginalized groups like girls and disabled children.Provide digital tools and internet access.
